## 🌍 Overview
Trevo AI Tourism Tools is a pioneering open-source ecosystem dedicated to the digital transformation of the tourism industry. While global tools are generic and expensive, Trevo provides specialized, AI-driven infrastructure for over **6,350 SMEs** (travel agencies, hotels, and operators) in the MENA region.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional tourism operations and the **$950B global experience economy** through accessible, scalable, and multilingual digital tools.

## 🎯 Impact Metrics (Quantified Value)
We don't just provide code; we deliver measurable results for tourism businesses:
- **+30% Increase in Conversion Rates:** Driven by AI-powered personalization and targeted marketing.
- **-15% Reduction in Ad Costs (CPC):** Optimized through tourism-specific data models.
- **94% Higher Booking Intent:** Achieved through our research-backed virtual preview integrations.
- **10+ Hours Saved Weekly:** Automating manual marketing and operational workflows.

## 🛠 Featured Tech: Geospatial VR Engine (Alpha)
The core of our current development is an open-standard **Geospatial VR Engine**. This tool bridges **Real-World Map Coordinates** with **Immersive 360° Content**, allowing travelers to:
1. **Virtual Map Tracking:** Visualize their journey on an interactive 3D map.
2. **Instant VR Previews:** "Visit" a destination or hotel room virtually before booking.
3. **Smart Cultural Integration:** Connecting historical and urban sites with AR/VR data layers.

## 🏗 Project Architecture
The repository is structured to evolve from a lightweight toolkit into a full-scale SaaS platform:
- `/api`: Flask-based backend for core business logic and AI processing.
- `/tools`: Specialized modules including ROI calculators and the AI Marketing Generator.
- `/website`: The frontend interface for our live MVP demo.
- `/docs`: Comprehensive guides for users and contributors.

## 🗺 Strategic Roadmap
- **Phase 1 (Current):** Core Marketing Automation & ROI analytics tools.
- **Phase 2 (Upcoming):** Launching the Open Geospatial VR Bridge and Mapbox integrations.
- **Phase 3:** FinTech layer for tourism micro-payments and multi-currency travel wallets.
- **Phase 4:** Global scaling to 100+ countries with localized AI dialects.
