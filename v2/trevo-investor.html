Risk Assessment

Key Risks & Mitigations

Market Adoption Speed

Medium

MENA tourism agencies may be slow to adopt AI tools. Digital literacy varies widely.

→ Founder-led sales + pilot pricing removes friction. 30-min demo converts skeptics.

Global Competitor Entry

Medium

HubSpot, Salesforce, or Google could launch tourism AI tools for MENA markets.

→ 18-24 month window + Arabic-first + founder network = hard to replicate fast.

Technical Execution Risk

High

Building an AI-powered SaaS platform requires strong technical talent in Egypt's competitive market.

→ 40% of pre-seed allocated to MVP dev. CTO hire is priority #1.

Currency & FX Risk

Medium

EGP volatility could affect pricing and margins for Egypt-based operations.

→ USD-denominated pricing. Multi-currency banking from day 1.

Tourism Market Shock

Medium

COVID-type events, regional instability, or airline disruptions could suppress tourism demand.

→ SaaS model is countercyclical — agencies need marketing help MORE in downturns.

Revenue Concentration

Low

Early reliance on a small number of pilot clients creates revenue concentration risk.

→ 10+ pilots from day 1. No single client >20% of revenue. Diversify by geography.
